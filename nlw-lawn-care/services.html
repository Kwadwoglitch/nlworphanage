Bike 4 Kids Project (BKP)

OBJECTIVE:We strive to empower children in underprivileged areas, such as Ghana, by providing bicycles as a reliable means of transportation to school. Our goal is to eliminate the barrier of long and challenging commutes, enabling students to attend classes regularly and consistently. By doing so, we aim to improve literacy rates and create brighter futures for these young learners, fostering a generation equipped with the tools for personal and community development.

Shelter for Shy Project (2SP)

We identify orphaned children through a thorough orphan census registration, providing a foundation for offering adoption opportunities and placing children in loving foster families. To ensure their well-being, we conduct psychosocial evaluations and offer interventions to monitor their emotional and psychological development. Additionally, we organize social activities that foster a sense of belonging, creating a family-like environment where these children can feel supported, valued, and loved despite their unfortunate circumstances.

Roof for Rookies Project (2RP)

In collaboration with like-minded NGOs, we raise funds and mobilize resources to build classrooms in the regions most in need. We also work to expand existing schools by adding new classrooms, ensuring that children who are currently learning in makeshift spaces under the sun can benefit from proper, sheltered learning environments. Through these efforts, we aim to provide every child with a safe, comfortable space to grow, learn, and thrive.

Impact and Future Goals

To date, we have distributed 300 bicycles, enabling more children to attend school regularly and focus on their education, rather than the challenges of long commutes. These bikes have made a significant difference in the lives of both the children and their families in Burkina Faso.

In November 2023, the Orphan Life Foundation plans to provide 50 bicycles to 10 schools in the Reo region. While 50 bikes may seem modest, it will greatly improve mobility for children who previously faced tremendous challenges in reaching school. Our goal is to raise funds for more bicycles to help orphaned and underprivileged children who currently walk 5 to 15 miles to school.

Since 2011, we’ve identified over 2,000 orphaned children, facilitated 17 adoptions, and placed 53 children in foster families. We’ve also supported 404 orphans in local shelters and raised over $1,700 to distribute clothing and shoes to orphanages and schools. Moving forward, we aim to raise $2,500 to support more schools and orphanages, continuing our mission to improve the lives of orphaned children and provide them with the opportunities they deserve.

Your support can make a lasting impact in these children's lives, helping them achieve their educational dreams and thrive.
