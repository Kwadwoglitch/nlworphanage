Making Cash Donations

  • Cash donations, whether in physical form or by cheque, are received by the Accounts Office. Upon receipt, a government-issued receipt bearing the name of the Home will be provided to the donor. All funds are then deposited into the Home’s account with the Bank of Ghana to ensure proper processing.

    Please make all cheques payable to the Children’s Home.

    Donations can be made in person at the Home’s premises or via bank transfer to our **Bank of Ghana Account**:

    Account Number: 1018631510027

    SWIFT Code: BAGHGHAC

    Your generous contribution will go a long way in supporting the children and programs at the Children’s Home. Thank you for your support!

Donating Materials

  • In addition to cash donations, we also accept materials such as food, clothes, medicines, and other essentials. These donations are received at the donations desk by our Stores Staff, who will issue a 'Thank You' card listing the donated items.

    The donated materials are used to support the daily needs of the children, including food, routine medicines for children with special needs, and covering essential expenses such as utility bills, hospital bills, tuition fees, maintenance, and transportation.

    To ensure transparency and accountability, the Home undergoes an annual audit by the Auditor General’s Department. This audit verifies all financial and material inflows, ensuring that donations are properly managed and used for their intended purposes.

    Your contributions help provide a better quality of life for the children and support the continued operations of the Home. Thank you for your generosity!

    • Adopt a Home Unit or Improve Home Facilities
    • Provide a Bus for School and Excursions
    • Support Facility Improvements
    • Provide Learning Materials and Care Essentials

      • Your support, whether through financial contributions, resources, or volunteering, will make a lasting difference in the lives of the children at N&LW Orphange Home. Thank you for helping us provide a brighter future for these children.
